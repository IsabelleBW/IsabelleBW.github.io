Latest Track

High Hazels

High Hazels are an English band made up of singer and guitarist James Leesley, lead guitarist Scott Howes, and brothers Paul and Anthony Barlow on bass guitar and drums. All members of the band grew up in Handsworth, a suburb of Sheffield where three of the members (James, Scott and Anthony) became friends in primary school and then later bonded over their shared passion for music.

The Time Sellers

The Time Sellers are 4 young lads from Chesterfield who in a very short space of time have created a real buzz in their native North of England that is starting to reach much much further afield. Comprising of cousins Jimmy and Adam Bull, Connor Pryde and Jacob Hall , The Time Sellers are passionate about what they do and refreshingly honest in how they do it.
